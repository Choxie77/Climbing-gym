Our Facilities

Discover our comprehensive climbing facilities designed for every skill level. From beginner-friendly walls to advanced training equipment, we provide everything you need for an exceptional climbing experience.

Climbing Areas

Multiple climbing zones designed for different styles and skill levels.

Top Rope & Lead Climbing

Rope Climbing Walls

Our main climbing area features 15-meter high walls with diverse route setting. From beginner-friendly top-rope routes to challenging lead climbing paths, we offer experiences for every climber.

  • 15-meter high climbing walls
  • Top rope and lead climbing routes
  • Auto-belay systems for solo climbing

Bouldering Area

Bouldering Zone

Our extensive bouldering area features over 200 square meters of climbing surface with problems ranging from beginner to advanced levels.

  • 200+ square meters of bouldering
  • Two-story mezzanine for advanced routes
  • Crash pads and safety equipment

Route Distribution

Interactive breakdown of our 80+ routes by difficulty level.

Training Zone

Professional training equipment to build strength and improve your climbing performance.

2016 Moonboard

The original Moonboard with a standardized 40-degree angle and LED system for training on the same problems as climbers worldwide.

  • • 40-degree overhang angle
  • • LED problem display system
  • • Standardized hold setup
  • • Access to global problem database

Campus Board

Traditional campus board for power training and contact strength development.

  • • Multiple rung sizes
  • • Adjustable board angle
  • • Progressive difficulty levels
  • • Power and technique training

Hangboard Area

Multiple hangboards for finger strength training and injury prevention.

  • • Various grip types
  • • Different hold depths
  • • Finger strength progression
  • • Injury rehabilitation tools

Spray Wall

Large overhanging wall with random holds for creative problem solving and endurance training.

  • • 30-degree overhang
  • • Random hold placement
  • • Endurance training
  • • Creative route setting
