Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

What should I bring for my first visit?

We recommend comfortable athletic clothing that allows free movement. All climbing equipment (shoes, harness, chalk) is available for rent at the gym. Bring a water bottle and towel - we have water stations and locker rooms with showers.

Do I need experience to climb at Main Wall?

Not at all! We welcome climbers of all levels, from complete beginners to advanced climbers. Our staff will provide a safety orientation, and we offer beginner classes to get you started. Auto-belay systems are available for solo climbing.

What is the age limit for climbing?

We welcome all ages! Children under 14 need adult supervision. Our weight limit is 120kg (265 lbs) for safety reasons. We have special youth programs and family climbing sessions available.

How much does it cost to climb?

Day passes start at 300 THB for adults and 200 THB for students. Equipment rental is 100 THB for shoes and harness. We also offer monthly memberships and class packages. Contact us for current pricing and promotions.

Is the gym air-conditioned?

Yes! We're the only fully air-conditioned climbing gym in Chiang Mai, providing a comfortable climbing experience even during hot weather. We also have a clean air filtration system for optimal air quality.
